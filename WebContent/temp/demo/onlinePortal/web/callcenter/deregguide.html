Card De-Registration Guide


The following steps shows how to deregister a card

  • Login to your account in website
  • Go to menu "Member Manage Cards"
  • You will see a list of cards that are already registered
  • Select the card you want to deregister and click "Deregister Card" button
  • You can even deregister you last card, but in that case you will loose all the benefits associate with this program
